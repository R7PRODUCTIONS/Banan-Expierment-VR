Gorilla Tag Fan Game

Move with your arms. Run the map. Escape the surprise.

Banan Expierament is a gorilla tag fan game with active updates and a good staff team. It uses a unique method of movement called "gorilla locomotion", where you move with your arms instead of a joystick. We feature awesome maps and cool surprises and escape.

FAQ

Got questions?

Is Banan Expierament free?

Yes, the base game is free to play. Optional cosmetics support development.

What headsets are supported?

Banan Expierament is available on Meta Quest via the Meta Store. SteamVR and PC VR are not supported.

How do updates work?

We announce changes in Discord and ship patches regularly. Join playtests to try features early.

Where can I report bugs or appeal a ban?

Use the #support channel in Discord for tickets. Our staff team responds quickly.
